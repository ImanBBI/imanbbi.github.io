Four of My Favorite Books

Book Name Cover Author Summary
The Intelligent Investor Benjamin Graham Classic on value investing (intrinsic value, “margin of safety,” long-term discipline). It’s still one of the most widely recommended investing books.
A Random Walk Down Wall Street Burton G. Malkiel A popular guide to markets explaining why consistently beating the market is hard, and how diversification + low-cost strategies can win over time.
Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind Yuval Noah Harari Big-picture history of how humans built societies, economies, and belief systems—also a major bestseller with huge readership.
Thinking, Fast and Slow Daniel Kahneman Explains how “fast” intuitive thinking and “slow” deliberate thinking shape our decisions, and how common biases can lead us to predictable mistakes.
